Why IEEE CS Pro

Organised by the IEEE Computer Society Bangalore Chapter, CS Pro 2026 brings the people who set direction and the people who build the future into the same room for thoughtful dialogue, considered perspective, and connections that shape what comes next.

The Format

Two gatherings, one morning

IEEE CS Pro 2026 unfolds as two connected sessions across a single morning.

Leadership Connect

Lead the conversation

It is our privilege to invite you to lead the conversation at an exclusive half-day Leadership Round Table. Join fellow leaders in exchanging practical insights, exploring shared challenges, and fostering collaborations that will help shape the future.

The Round Tables

Three conversations that matter

The morning centres on three round tables running in parallel. Across them, leaders explore the forces reshaping organisations today, from bold transformation and the future of talent to the responsible rise of AI, in candid, unscripted exchange rather than scripted panels.

Specific round-table topics and table allocations will be shared with confirmed participants about a week ahead of the event.
